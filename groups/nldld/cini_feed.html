comfort in isolation

We have created a collaborative video made up of five parts by five different musicians, who were only allowed to see a 10 second snippet of the pieces that had gone before them. We had an agreed starting stimulus of what we were finding comfort in during isolation and we each had 5 days to produce a 1 minute clip, with no other limitations.

In addition to our video, we wanted to open up this project to others and asked our friends and family to film/record something that they were also finding comfort within during this time. See our Instagram page below
